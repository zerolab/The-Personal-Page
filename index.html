“Woke up one day and decided to live”

Dan Braghis is a webtimizer, experimenter and Drupal developer working for The Browser. While keeping very busy these days, he shares occasional bits and pieces of internet boredom on Tumblr, some code on GitHub, and stashes lots of code to internal repositories.

The past few years he did a good share of odd projects, but plenty of great ones too with his mates at Tfab. From polymeme.com, the now defunct meme-tracker for the polymath out there, to keeping an eye on More Intelligent Life or trying to make the world a better place by helping map threatened or harassed bloggers on Threatened Voices, dip my toe in the subject of transparency on Technology for Transparency Network or tinker on 2 Water: Wine charity.

He moved recently to Oxfordshire, UK where he lives happily with his wonderful girlfriend Sylwia and enjoys the geek environment
